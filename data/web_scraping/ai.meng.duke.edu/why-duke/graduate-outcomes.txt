# Graduate Outcomes

Nearly 8 in 10 start work or further education within 6 months of graduation

We're proud that nearly 8 in 10 recent Duke Engineering graduates started work
or further education within six months of graduation.

Duke AIPI students go into some of the most in-demand and exciting job roles
available, such as ML Engineers, AI Engineers, Data Scientists, and Data
Engineers.

Our best-in-class student experience includes access to a dedicated career
services team, networking opportunities, and employer recruitment events.

## Selected Graduate Employers

Our students join organizations leading the way in applying AI/ML to industry

  * OpenAI
  * Target's AI Lab
  * Grubhub
  * Door Dash

  * Duke Health
  * ETS AI Labs
  * And many more
