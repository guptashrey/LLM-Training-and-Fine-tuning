## You've got the career vision and a great foundation

**Now you need the advanced knowledge, skills and experience to make your
vision a reality.** The Duke Master of Engineering program will deepen your
understanding of technology and help you develop the business leadership and
management expertise you need to succeed in your career. Duke's MEng program
will sharpen your critical thinking and communications skills, and make you
competitive in today's high tech fields.
