## Important Notes

  * Applicants should submit materials as early as possible before the
    deadlines listed above
  * To be considered in a round, the **application _and_ all supporting
    documents** must be received by the date indicated
  * In some cases, a Duke Master of Engineering program may postpone a
    decision to the next round
  * Admitted students may request to defer admission to a future term—contact
    Duke for more information

_Questions? Contact the Admissions Team at_ pratt_masters@duke.edu.
