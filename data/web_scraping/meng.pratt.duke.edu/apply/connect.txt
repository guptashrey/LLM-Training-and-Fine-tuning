# Connect With Us

Do you want to learn more about the Master of Engineering program? Connecting
with our Admissions Team is a great way to find out if Duke is the right fit
for you. We look forward to meeting you soon!

## Contact Us

Have a question about the program or admissions? Contact the **Admissions &
Recruiting team** at pratt_masters@duke.edu.

## Virtually

Visit with us from anywhere in the world! You will have the opportunity to
connect in real-time with our admissions team, department directors, and
program staff through webinars, live Q&A sessions, and 1:1 sessions. RSVP
here.
