# Minimum Application Requirements

The program does not require a minimum GPA or a minimum score on the GRE or
TOEFL/IELTS. The program does not require work experience. View our admission
profile below to see the mid-50% range in test scores and undergraduate grade
point average. Obtaining scores within these ranges is not a guarantee of
admission.

### Admissions Profile

The Duke Master of Engineering (MEng) program does not require a minimum GPA
or a minimum score on the GRE or TOEFL. The program does not require work
experience. Average GRE, TOEFL and grade-point averages of recently admitted
Duke MEng applicants were*:

  * GRE Quantitative: 163-169
  * GRE Verbal: 152-161
  * UGPA: 3.4-3.7

* Mid-50% range
