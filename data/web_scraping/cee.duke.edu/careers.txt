### Committed to Diversity, Equity & Inclusion

Duke University and Duke Engineering aspire to create a community built on
collaboration, innovation, creativity, and belonging.

Our collective success depends on the robust exchange of ideas—an exchange
that is best when the rich diversity of our perspectives, backgrounds, and
experiences flourishes.

To achieve this exchange, it is essential that all members of the community
feel secure and welcome, that the contributions of all individuals are
respected, and that all voices are heard.

All members of our community have a responsibility to uphold these values.
