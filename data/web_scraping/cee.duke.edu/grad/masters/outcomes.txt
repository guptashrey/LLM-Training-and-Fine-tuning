# Career Outcomes for Duke CEE Master's Graduates

## Breadcrumb

  1. Home
  2. Graduate
  3. Masters

More than half find full-time employment or pursue more education within 6
months of graduation

Most Duke CEE's master's graduates go on to leading engineering, construction,
consulting and technology firms. Others pursue further study at Duke and other
leading academic institutions.
