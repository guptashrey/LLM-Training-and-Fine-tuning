# Master's Study at Duke CEE

## Breadcrumb

  1. Home
  2. Graduate

We offer significant scholarship support to our master's students

At Duke CEE, you will be part of a highly regarded program that is ranked
among the very best. Studying for a master's degree at Duke prepares you for
leadership in industry or future doctoral studies.

## Benefits for Duke CEE Master's Students

  * **Scholarships** —for competitive applicants, typically, $20,000-$30,000
  * **Flexible curriculum** —with choices of concentration
  * **Interdisciplinary training** —in engineering, science and management
  * **Project-based courses** —Duke research translated into experiential
    learning
  * **Dedicated career services** —plus an industry advisory board and global
    network of Duke graduates

## Scholarships

Significant support for competitive applicants. Typical scholarships range

### $20,000-$30,000

## Degree programs

### Master of Science (MS) in Civil & Environmental Engineering

  * An advanced engineering degree, comprising technical coursework and a
    thesis or project
  * 1 to 2 years to earn
  * Excellent foundation for a career in research or technology development

Choose a study track:

  * Computational Mechanics and Scientific Computing
  * Environmental Geomechanics and Geophysics
  * Systems, Risk and Decision
  * Hydrology and Fluid Dynamics
  * Environmental Health Engineering

explore Ms

### Master of Engineering (MEng) in Civil Engineering

  * A career-focused degree, that includes strong technical and business
    coursework
  * Concludes with a required industry internship
  * Emphasizes business and management skills

Choose a concentration area:

  * Computational Engineering
  * Systems Engineering and Optimization
  * Geo-Systems

Explore Civil

### Master of Engineering (MEng) in Environmental Engineering

  * A career-focused degree that includes technical and business coursework
  * Concludes with a required industry internship
  * Draws on faculty from both Duke's Pratt School of Engineering and its
    Nicholas School of the Environment

Pick a specialization:

  * Ecohydrology and Environmental Fluid Dynamics
  * Environmental Process Engineering
  * Environmental Engineering and Public Policy

Explore Environmental

### Master of Engineering (MEng) in Risk Engineering

  * A career-focused degree that includes technical and business coursework
  * Concludes with a required industry internship
  * Focuses on skills in engineering and statistical decision theory

Prepare for a career in:

  * Insurance risk assessment
  * Engineering consulting
  * Infrastructure analysis

Explore Risk

### Master of Engineering (MEng) in Computational Mechanics & Scientific
Computing

  * A career-focused degree that includes technical and business coursework
  * Concludes with a required industry internship
  * Focuses on computational algorithms for model-based simulation and design

Prepare for a career in:

  * Additive manufacturing
  * Engineering mechanics
  * Engineering consulting

Explore Comp. Mech.
