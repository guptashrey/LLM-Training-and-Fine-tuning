# ASCE Outstanding Senior Award

## Breadcrumb

  1. Home
  2. About
  3. Awards & Honors

_Presented annually to a graduating civil engineering senior or seniors in
recognition of exceptional, positive impact on the student chapter of ASCE_

Each recipient, which is selected by the faculty and sponsored by the North
Carolina Section of the American Society of Civil Engineer, receives:

  * A certificate at the North Carolina Section's annual meeting in the fall
  * First-year associate member dues in ASCE
