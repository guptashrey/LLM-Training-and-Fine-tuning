# Data & the Duke Engineer

The explosion of data in the information age promises to give humanity an
unprecedented ability to discover new things and make better decisions. From
improving health to safeguarding the environment, the potential impact is
huge—and so is the need for people who can lead the way.

That’s why Duke Engineering is investing in our students, our faculty and our
infrastructure to create a **signature undergraduate experience** that gives
every student knowledge of data analytics—and **expanding our team of faculty
thought leaders** to inspire and mentor them.

We're proud to share our ambitious plans and exciting progress, and invite you
to contact us to get involved.

## Data & the Student Experience

Creating data-savvy students across the spectrum

  * **New signature course** gives every Duke Engineering undergraduate the
    power to harness data science
  * **Data+**: Duke’s award-winning approach to education for the data age
    partners students and faculty with clients from industry, healthcare &
    beyond
  * **Empowering graduate students**: “Data Expeditions” offers a unique
    professional development opportunity for students like Chris Tralie, a PhD
    student in Duke ECE
  * **Engaging industry**: Real-world problem-solving for students in Duke
    Master of Engineering Management’s Data & Decisions Track
  * **250,000 learners & counting**: Duke’s online classes through Coursera
    bring data analytics education to professionals everywhere
