# Results: 2022 Student Design Fair

April 28, 2022

Winners of the 2022 Student Design Fair held at Duke's Pratt School of
Engineering

## Biggest Difference (tie)

Will make the most difference to those for whom the device is intended

  * **Project Team #37** — A low-cost colostomy bag for those in low- to
    medium-resourced settings
    * Joanna Peng
    * Alanna Manfredini
    * Amy Guan
    * Darienne Rogers
  * **Project Team #13** — A way to use CRISPR-Cas13/Cas12 lateral flow assay
    to diagnose both drug-susceptible and drug-resistant strains of
    tuberculosis
    * Sean Li
    * Harshitha Venugopal Lavanya
    * Michael Tian
    * Simone Wall

## Biggest Breakthrough

New technology, or use of existing technology, that makes a step-change

**Project Team #11** — A way to alter the behavior of diseased brain cells to
cure Alzheimer's Disease

  * Lucy Zhang
  * Ethan Nicklow
  * Joy Fu
  * Aditya Gopalan
  * Jackson Adams

## Best Use of Analytical Skills

Robust use of calculations that lead to improved design or function

**Project Team #22** — A small-scale vertical landing rocket

  * JD Inge
  * Everett Werner
  * Justin Nickelsburg
  * Joanna Feaster
  * Muazzam Khan-Noorpuri
  * Kartikay Goel
  * Mario Munoz

## Most Creative & Engaging

Captures the attention of users; pleasing to behold

**Project Team #30** — Detachable rollerskates

  * Anna Song

## Most Likely to be Adopted (tie) **  
**

The target audience is likely to use this in place of what is currently
available

  * **Project Team #43** — Custom website and text bot for SKANSKA to schedule
    deliveries of goods to construction worksites
    * Will Denton
    * Vivek Tarapara
    * Ken Kalin
    * Dez Kecker
    * Del Cudjoe
  * **Project Team #9** — A worksite foam cutter for people with disabilities
    * Laine Beckler
    * Drew Genuit
