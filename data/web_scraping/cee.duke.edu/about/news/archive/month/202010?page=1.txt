jump-start the body’s innate healing abilities

### Undergraduate Duke Engineering Design Teams Win National Prizes

their projects, even years later

### Calculating the Risk of COVID-19 Exposure

App lets users set their own parameters, make better-informed decisions

### Duke Names Mechanical Engineering & Materials Science Department for
Benefactor Thomas Lord

MEMS department and chair position named to recognize long-term contributions
of former LORD Corporation chair Thomas Lord and president Donald Alstadt

### New Pathway for Students to Double Major in BME and Computer Science

Duke BME formalizes a new double major, offering a curriculum at the
intersection of computer science and biomedical engineering

### Duke Engineering Announces New Minor in Machine Learning & Artificial
Intelligence

To help meet the growing demand for professionals trained machine learning and
AI theory and practice, Duke Engineering is now offering a Minor in Machine
Learning & Artificial Intelligence to undergraduates across the university
