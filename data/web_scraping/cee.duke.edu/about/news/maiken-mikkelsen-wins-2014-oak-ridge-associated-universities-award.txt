# Maiken Mikkelsen Wins 2014 Oak Ridge Associated Universities Award

July 28, 2014

Assistant professor of electrical and computer engineering and physics at Duke

Maiken Mikkelsen, an assistant professor of electrical and computer
engineering and physics, and Amanda Hargrove, an assistant professor of
chemistry, have been selected by Oak Ridge Associated Universities (ORAU) to
receive 2014 Ralph E. Powe Junior Faculty Enhancement Awards. The award
provides a one-year, $5,000 grant to fund research by junior faculty at ORAU
member institutions to enrich the research and professional growth of young
faculty. Mikkelsen’s research focuses on nanophysics and experimental
condensed matter physics.
