## Education

  * Ph.D. University of California - Santa Barbara, 2009

## Research Interests

Quantum nanophotonics, plasmonics, light-matter interactions in artificially
structured nanoscale materials, hybrid molecular-scale materials and spin
phenomena in the solid state

    Mikkelsen. “Ultrafast pyroelectric photodetection with on-chip spectral
