  * Daubechies, I., R. Devore, N. Dym, S. Faigenbaum-Golovin, S. Z. Kovalsky,
    K. C. Lin, J. Park, G. Petrova, and B. Sober. “Neural Network

    https://doi.org/10.1007/s00365-021-09548-z.

    Ingrid Daubechies, Pier Luigi Dragotti, and Miguel R. D. Rodrigues. “Mixed
