# Duke Seminars in Neural Engineering: Neurophysiological readouts of pathway
modulation during deep brain stimulation therapy

##  Thursday, February 2, 2023 – 12:00PM to 1:00PM

## Presenter

Matt Johnson, University of Minnesota

The Center for Neural Engineering & Neurotechnology focuses on using
engineering techniques to understand the mechanisms of and develop advanced
approaches to electrical stimulation of the nervous system to restore function
to individuals with neurological impairment or injury.
