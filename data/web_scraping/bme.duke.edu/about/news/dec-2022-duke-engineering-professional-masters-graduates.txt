# Making Real Impact: Duke Engineering Professional Master's Graduates

February 2, 2023

Duke Engineering's December 2022 professional master's graduates are prepared
for careers of deep meaning and broad impact

Helping improve how household robots are made. Applying financial technology
skills to managing multi-billion-dollar university endowments. Building an AI
chatbot to serve the clients of a major Wall Street firm. These are just a few
of the individual stories from dozens of recent Duke Engineering master's
graduates.

The December 2022 graduates of the professional master's degree programs of
Duke Engineering's Institute for Enterprise Engineering entered the
marketplace ready to make positive impacts for themselves, employers and
society.

Scroll and click on their names to meet some of our December professional
master's graduates.
