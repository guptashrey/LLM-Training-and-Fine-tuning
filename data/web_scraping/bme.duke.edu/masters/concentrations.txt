# Master's Degree Concentrations

## Breadcrumb

  1. Home
  2. Masters

Customize your master's degree with a concentration or track

Duke BME faculty members are engaged in a wide range of efforts to improve
human health through research. Our master's students can select courses to
concentrate on an area of faculty research focus.

The courses listed below are just _a few examples_.

At Duke BME, you have all of Duke to choose from. Working with your advisor,
may count relevant courses offered by other Duke departments and Duke
University Medical Center.

  * BME 506 Measurement and Control of Cardiac Electrical Events
  * BME 507 Cardiovascular System Engineering, Disease, and Therapy
  * BME 510 Bayesian Analysis in Biomedical Engineering
