### Career Paths

A master's degree in biomedical engineering from Duke Engineering helps open
opportunities for future success. Graduates of our BME program have gone on to
careers at elite academic institutions and at leading biotech firms.

  * Most Duke BME master's grads go on to **careers in the public or private
    sector**
  * About 25 percent of Duke BME MS grads enter **PhD programs**

### Total Annual Salary Survey

  * Number of Graduates Reporting: 28

  * 25th Percentile: $69,750
