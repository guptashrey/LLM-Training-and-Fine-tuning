## Duke BME Master's Certificates

### Biomedical Data Science

The master's Certificate in Biomedical Data Science gives biomedical engineers
a competitive edge in industry and academia, translating biomedical data into
actionable health insights.

See program details »

### Biotechnology

The master's Certificate in Biotechnology provides Duke BME students with in-
depth knowledge of cutting-edge techniques for modeling, analyzing and
designing molecular and cellular systems.

See program details »

### Medical Device Design

Duke BME Master's students who earn the Certificate in Medical Device Design
are well-prepared for a career in the medical device industry.

See program details »

### Neural Engineering

Students who earn a master's Certificate in Neural Engineering gain practical
experience toward the discovery of new applications of tools, approaches and
skills to diagnose, study, and treat disorders and diseases of the human
nervous system.

See program details »

## Affiliated Master's Certificates

### Nanoscience

Duke BME participates in the university-wide Graduate Certificate Program in
Nanoscience—which provides graduate students an interdisciplinary education in
the broad areas of nanoscience and nanomaterials.

See program details »

### Photonics

Duke BME participates in the Duke Photonics Certificate—which provides a venue
for learning more about this path to interdisciplinary research and technology
development.

See program details »
