# Morton Friedman

## Breadcrumb

  1. Home

Professor Emeritus of Biomedical Engineering

Fluid and wall mechanical factors in atherosclerosis, cineangiographic and
vascular ultrasound image processing, relation between arterial geometry and
disease risk
