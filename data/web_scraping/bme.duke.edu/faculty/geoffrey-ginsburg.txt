# Geoffrey Steven Ginsburg

## Breadcrumb

  1. Home
  2. Faculty

Adjunct Professor in the Department of Medicine

Dr. Geoffrey S. Ginsburg's research interests are in the development of novel
paradigms for developing and translating genomic information into medical
practice and the integration of personalized medicine into health care.

## Appointments and Affiliations

  * Adjunct Professor in the Department of Medicine
  * Professor in Pathology
  * Professor of Biostatistics and Bioinformatics
  * Professor in the School of Nursing
  * Member of the Duke Cancer Institute
