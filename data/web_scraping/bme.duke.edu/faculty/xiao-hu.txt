# Xiao Hu

## Breadcrumb

  1. Home
  2. Faculty

Ann Henshaw Gardiner Distinguished Professor of Nursing

## Appointments and Affiliations

  * Professor in the School of Nursing
  * Professor in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering
  * Professor of Biomedical Engineering
  * Professor of Biostatistics and Bioinformatics
  * Associate Professor in Surgery
  * Professor in Neurology

## Contact Information

  * **Office Location:** 2 Pine Top Place, Durham, NC 27705
  * **Office Phone:** (310) 498-6136
  * **Email Address:** xiao.hu@duke.edu

## Education

  * Ph.D. University of California - Los Angeles, 2004

## Research Interests

Biomedical signal processing (ECG, EEG, PPG, ICP, cerebral blood flow
velocity), machine learning in healthcare, mathematical modeling of
physiological systems, brain monitoring.

## Courses Taught

  * BME 790L: Advanced Topics with the Lab for Graduate Students in Biomedical
    Engineering
  * BME 791: Graduate Independent Study
  * NURSING 574: Directed Scholarship
  * NURSING 724: Data Analytics II
  * NURSING 922: Special Readings in School of Nursing

## Representative Publications

  * Sunghan Kim, K. A., F. Scalzo, M. Bergsneider, P. Vespa, N. Martin, and N.
    Xiao Hu. “Noninvasive Intracranial Pressure Assessment Based on a Data-
    Mining Approach Using a Nonlinear Mapping Function.” Ieee Transactions on
    Biomedical Engineering 59, no. 3 (March 2012): 619–26.
    https://doi.org/10.1109/tbme.2010.2093897.
  * Yang, Li, Mingxi Zhao, Chenglin Peng, Xiao Hu, Hua Feng, and Zhong Ji.
    “Waveform descriptor for pulse onset detection of intracranial pressure
    signal.” Medical Engineering & Physics 34, no. 2 (March 2012): 179–86.
    https://doi.org/10.1016/j.medengphy.2011.07.008.
  * Scalzo, Fabien, Shadnaz Asgari, Sunghan Kim, Marvin Bergsneider, and Xiao
    Hu. “Bayesian tracking of intracranial pressure signal morphology.”
    Artificial Intelligence in Medicine 54, no. 2 (February 2012): 115–23.
    https://doi.org/10.1016/j.artmed.2011.08.007.
  * Asgari, Shadnaz, Paul Vespa, Marvin Bergsneider, and Xiao Hu. “Lack of
    consistent intracranial pressure pulse morphological changes during
    episodes of microdialysis lactate/pyruvate ratio increase.” Physiological
    Measurement 32, no. 10 (October 1, 2011): 1639–51.
    https://doi.org/10.1088/0967-3334/32/10/011.
  * Asgari, Shadnaz, Marvin Bergsneider, Robert Hamilton, Paul Vespa, and Xiao
    Hu. “Consistent Changes in Intracranial Pressure Waveform Morphology
    Induced by Acute Hypercapnic Cerebral Vasodilatation.” Neurocritical Care
    15, no. 1 (August 2011): 55–62. https://doi.org/10.1007/s12028-010-9463-x.
