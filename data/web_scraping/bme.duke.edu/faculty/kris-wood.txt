# Kris Cameron Wood

## Breadcrumb

  1. Home
  2. Faculty

Associate Professor of Pharmacology and Cancer Biology

Our laboratory uses genomic and pharmacological approaches to understand how
tumor dependencies are shaped by cell intrinsic factors, environmental
factors, and drug treatments during the dynamic process of tumor evolution. To
learn more, please visit our laboratory website:
https://sites.duke.edu/woodlab/.
