# Master's Degrees

Prepare for success in industry, research or academia

Duke BME is a leader—highly ranked among the very best in offering career-
ready master's degrees in biomedical engineering. Our degree programs include:

  * Master of Science (MS) in Biomedical Engineering »

  * Master of Engineering (MEng) in Biomedical Engineering »

  * Master of Engineering (MEng) in Medical Technology Design »

  * MD + Masters »

## The Duke Difference

We're invested in your success.

In addition to access to leading faculty, state-of-the-science facilities, and
an inclusive, supportive community, our master's students enjoy a high quality
of life in our hometown of Durham, NC. _Compare the cost of living_

We provide exceptional student experiences and support, including career
services—featuring workshops and one-on-one coaching.

Duke BME graduates go on to do great things. Within six months of graduating,
more than 80% of our grads are employed or furthering their educations.
