# Graduate Outcomes

Nearly 9 in 10 start work or further education within 6 months of graduation

We're proud that nearly 9 in 10 recent graduates started work or further
education within six months of graduation.

Engineering master's students at Duke have access to a dedicated career
services team, networking opportunities and employer recruitment events.
