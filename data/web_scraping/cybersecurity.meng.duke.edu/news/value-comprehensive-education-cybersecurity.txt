# The Value of a Comprehensive Education in Cybersecurity

September 24, 2020

WATCH: A conversation about cybersecurity education with retired US Air Force
Maj. Gen. Brett Williams

In this conversation with Jimmie Lenz, director of the Duke Cybersecurity
Master of Engineering Program, Brett Williams, Maj Gen USAF (Ret), a Duke
Computer Science graduate, discusses the need for more robust cybersecurity
education, based on his experience in both the public and private sectors.

"The opportunity to leverage individuals like Brett in the development of this
new graduate degree at Duke's Pratt School of Engineering has been priceless,"
Lenz said.
