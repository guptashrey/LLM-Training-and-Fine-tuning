# US CISA: Apply by Jan. 6 for PAID student internships

December 11, 2020

CISA is seeking undergraduate and graduate students for PAID internships.
Application Deadline is Jan 6, 2021

The U.S. Cybersecurity & Infrastructure Security Agency (CISA) is seeking
undergraduate and graduate students for PAID internships.

These internships are for student trainees in Cyber/IT at the **_GS-1/2_**,
**_GS-3/4_**, and **_GS-5/7_** levels. All interested students need to apply
via the link provided below and complete any associated assessments no later
than **January 6, 2021**.

Links to apply for **_PAID internships in cyber_** :

  * **_GS-1/2_**
  * **_GS-3/4_**
  * **_GS-5/7_**

Past interns have provided support on meaningful projects that include
developing a domain name system (DNS) playbook for threat-hunting tactics,
analyzing and developing social media strategy for a cyber team, building the
first sector-specific report highlighting cyber hygiene vulnerability data,
and analyzing a cybersecurity publication team’s products to identify gaps in
mitigation information.

Learn more about CISA careers at **_cisa.gov/careers_**.
