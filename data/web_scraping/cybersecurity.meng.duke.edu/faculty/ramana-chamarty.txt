# Ramanarao Chamarty

Adjunct Assistant Professor in the Pratt School of Engineering

Ramana Chamarty is Chief Architect, Head of Enterprise Cybersecurity
Architecture at Fidelity investment and is responsible for technical
leadership, vision and architectural strategy with oversight over 200+
security capabilities. He also serves as an adjunct faculty at University of
North Carolina, Chapel Hill teaching intermediate and advanced database
management systems at the School of Information and Library Sciences and
Adjunct instructor at North Carolina School of Science and Mathematics
teaching introduction to cybersecurity. Previously he worked at Temple
University as faculty and Director of Identity and Access Management.

His expertise includes various topics in cybersecurity and specializes in
Identity Management, provisioning and governance, authentication,
authorization, fraud prevention, Identity proofing and matching, Cloud
Directory services, Access Control and Management, API security, Privileged
Access Management and IAM NIST Controls. He has been a speaker at
international and national conferences and has given talks on various security
and advanced database topics. His areas interest includes cyber hygiene, zero
trust in action, cyber analytics, decentralized identity, persona centric IAM,
and adaptive access factoring risk.
