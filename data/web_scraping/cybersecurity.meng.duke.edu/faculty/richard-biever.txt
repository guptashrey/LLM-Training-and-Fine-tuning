# Richard Biever

Adjunct Assistant Professor in the Pratt School of Engineering

Richard Biever is Duke University's chief information security officer and
director of identity management.

He joined Duke in February 2011 after previously holding positions with the
Georgia Institute of Technology's Office of Information Technology and with
Hewlett Packard.

Richard is an experienced security professional with SANS GIAC Certified
Enterprise Defender (GSED) and Certified Information Systems Security
Professional (CISSP) credentials. He holds a bachelor’s degree in political
science from the University of Georgia and a master’s degree in international
relations from Georgia State University.
