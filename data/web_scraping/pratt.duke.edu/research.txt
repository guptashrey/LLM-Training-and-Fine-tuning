# High-Impact Research

Duke Engineering is a leader in defining and advancing **high-impact fields**
that tie to grand challenges for engineering and society.  
  
We rank #12 among the top 25 U.S. engineering schools in per-faculty research
funding (U.S. News & World Report data).

New external research funding reached **a record $105 million** in FY21—the
figure nearly doubling over the last decade. Duke Engineering is the home to
12 major externally funded research centers.
