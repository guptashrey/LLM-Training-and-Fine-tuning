# Book Club Meeting: 'The Signal and the Noise" by Nate Silver

Mar 30

_This event has passed._

##

Thursday, March 30, 2023 - 2:30pm to 3:30pm

The Signal and the Noise: Why So Many Predictions Fail-But Some Don't by Nate
Silver - 2:30-3:30 pm, Thursday, March 30; Duke Gardens Amphitheater Join us
to discuss this book! Political forecaster Nate Silver reveals how we can
develop better foresight in an uncertain world. From the stock market to the
poker table, from earthquakes to the economy, he takes us on an enthralling
insider's tour of the high-stakes world of forecasting, showing how we can use
information in a smarter way amid a noise of data-and make better predictions
in our own lives. Download the e-book:
https://find.library.duke.edu/catalog/DUKE005832554
