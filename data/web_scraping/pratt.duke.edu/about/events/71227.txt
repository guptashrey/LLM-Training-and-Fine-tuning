# Let's Talk: Maximizing Your Duke Savings Benefits

Mar 7

_This event has passed._

##

Tuesday, March 7, 2023 - 4:00pm to 5:30pm

## Presenter

Experts from Duke and Fidelity

Pratt Faculty and Staff: Are you maximizing your use of Duke's outstanding
benefits and retirement savings plans? Attend a special session on March 7 to
learn tips to: Provide education for yourself and your children; Create
financial protection for yourself and your family; Build wealth and support a
fulfilling retirement. This Pratt Community event is a wonderful chance to
hear from the Duke Benefits team and Fidelity Investments and to ask
questions. Submit a question in advance to eileen.kramer@duke.edu. Your
question can be presented to our experts anonymously.
