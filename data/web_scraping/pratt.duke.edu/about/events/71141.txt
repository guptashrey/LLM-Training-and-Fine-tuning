# Pratt HR Professional Development Series: Performance Management

Mar 7

_This event has passed._

##

Tuesday, March 7, 2023 - 12:00pm to 2:00pm

## Presenter

Staff & Labor Relations

Registration: https://duke.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_8CdunYixcRswjGu  
  
Participation limited to 28 attendees  
  
Topic: Performance Management

  * Orientation & Trial Period goal setting (defining expectations)
  * Importance of periodic reviews during first 90 days
  * Best practices in completing the Self-Assessment and PEP
  * Ongoing performance management
  * Duke policy / process for warnings, write-ups, documentation of
    communication between management and the employee, etc
  * When to follow up with Corrective Action/PIP
  * Having performance discussions
  * Documentation of behaviors and performance
