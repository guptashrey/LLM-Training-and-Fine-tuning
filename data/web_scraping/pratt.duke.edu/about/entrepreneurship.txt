# Duke Engineering Entrepreneurship

Building a better world through innovation—Developing lifelong innovators

Duke Engineering Entrepreneurship (EngEn) leverages university talent to build
a better world through technological innovation.

EngEn develops:

  * New, tech-driven startup companies
  * Lifelong innovators who deliver high-impact products

We do this through an experienced team of entrepreneurs working as
instructors, mentors and leaders in an array of educational opportunities,
programs and resources for faculty, students and staff.

Learn about the projects completed by our COVID-19 Engineering Response Team.

Go to engen.duke.edu

> "EngEn is an engine for innovation—driving the cycle of problem
> identification, solution development and the robust launch of new ventures."
>
> **Ken Gall**  
>  Associate Dean of Entrepreneurship
