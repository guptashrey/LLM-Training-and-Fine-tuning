### Truskey Named Editor-in-Chief of New Biomedical Engineering Journal

George Truskey, the R. Eugene and Susie E. Goodson Professor of Biomedical
Engineering and senior associate dean of the Pratt School of Engineering at
Duke University, has been named the editor-in-chief of the new Elsevier
journal Current Opinion in Biomedical Engineering.
