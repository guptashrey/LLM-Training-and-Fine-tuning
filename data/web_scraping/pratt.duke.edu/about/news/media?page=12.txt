### Being An Effective Chief Information Security Officer

Featuring Dave Chatterjee, Richard Biever

Dave Chatterjee and Richard Biever, who are both information-security experts

### Duke Researchers Working On Wearable Tech That Could Help With Variety of
Medical Issues

Featuring Jessilyn Dunn

### Duke University Engineers Developing Special 3D Printer for Bone
Reconstruction

Featuring Ken Gall

EngEn director Ken Gall talks about the growing field of 3D-printed titanium
biomedical implants that support bones while helping them heal.

Pathology News | July 19, 2021

### A Smartphone Pathology Platform to Characterize Tumor Cytology and HER2
Expression in Breast Cancer

Featuring Ashutosh Chilkoti

BME Professor Ashutosh Chilkoti and his lab has developed a multimodal mobile
platform called the EpiView-D4 for point-of-care characterization of the
cellular morphology and molecular expression of clinically relevant biomarkers
in fine-needle aspiration specimens from breast tumors.

Los Angeles Times | July 18, 2021

### Column: If the Weeks Seem to Be Passing Faster or Slower Than Usual,
You’re Not Alone. It’s COVID Time

Featuring Adrian Bejan

MEMS Professor Adrian Bejan says that during the early days of the pandemic,
many people experienced a slowing of time because we were having a whole new
range of experiences and behaviors and our daily lives felt unfamiliar.

including Ken Gall, Shyni Varghese and Matthew Becker.
