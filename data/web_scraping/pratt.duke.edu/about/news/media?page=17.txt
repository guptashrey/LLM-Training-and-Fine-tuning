### Painkiller-Dispensing Patch Could Be an Alternative to Opioids

Featuring Matthew Becker

MEMS Professor Matthew Becker develops a prototype polymer patch loaded with a
non-opioid drug that blocks pain and inflammation enzymes and erodes slowly in
wet environments, gradually releasing its pharmaceutical payload as it does
so.
