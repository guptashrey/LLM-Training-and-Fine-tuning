## Directions

### From Greensboro and Points West

  * Via 1-85 North: Exit onto N.C. 147 (Durham Freeway).
  * Take first exit off Durham Freeway onto 15-501 S. toward Chapel Hill.
  * Stay in right lane, and take second exit (#107), marked Duke Univ. West
    Campus, to N.C. 751.
  * Turn left onto N.C. 751 S., and go under the freeway.
  * At third traffic light (Science Drive) take a left. Continue past
    Towerview Rd.
  * Turn right into the Visitor Parking section to the north of the Chapel.
    Parking is available outdoors or in the Bryan Center PGIV parking garage.
  * The Engineering Buildings are located beyond the Science Drive traffic
    circle.

### From Richmond and Points North

  * Via I-85 South: in Durham, take the left-lane exit (#174B) for U.S. 15-501
    South Bypass - Duke University/Chapel Hill.
  * Continue on U.S. 15-501 to the fourth exit (#107), marked Duke Univ. West
    Campus, a right-lane exit for N.C. 751.
  * Turn left onto N.C. 751 S. Go under the freeway.
  * At third traffic light (Science Drive) take a left. Continue past
    Towerview Rd.
  * Turn right Into the Visitor Parking section to the north of the Chapel.
    Parking is available outdoors or in the Bryan Center PGIV parking garage.
  * The Engineering Buildings are located beyond the Science Drive traffic
    circle.

### From RDU Airport, Raleigh and Points East

  * Via I-40 West: Exit onto the Durham Freeway (N.C. 147). Take Durham
    Freeway to Chapel Hill St. exit (#13).
  * Take right on Chapel Hill St. Continue straight on Chapel Hill St. until
    it turns into Duke University Rd.
  * Move to far right lane. Drive past Chapel Dr. and then turn right onto
    Towerview Rd.
  * Drive past Cameron Stadium to Science Drive stop light.
  * Turn right onto Science Drive.
  * Turn right Into the Visitor Parking section to the north of the Chapel.
    Parking is available outdoors or in the Bryan Center PGIV parking garage.
  * The Engineering Buildings are located beyond the Science Drive traffic
    circle.

### From Chapel Hill

  * Via Us. 15-501 North. Take 15-501 North, marked Duke Univ./Med Ctr. Do not
    take 15-501 Business.
  * Take exit for N.C. 751 (#107) marked Duke University West Campus.
  * Turn right onto N.C. 751 S.(Cameron Blvd.).
  * At second traffic light (Science Drive), take a left. Continue past
    Towerview Rd.
  * Turn right Into the Visitor Parking section to the north of the Chapel for
    parking. Parking is available outdoors or in the Bryan Center PGIV parking
    garage.
  * The Engineering Buildings are located beyond the Science Drive traffic
    circle.
