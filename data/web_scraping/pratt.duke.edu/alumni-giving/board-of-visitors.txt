# Board of Visitors

An advisory board whose members include some of our most distinguished alumni
and friends

The Duke Engineering Board of Visitors provides advice and counsel in long-
range planning, academic programs, fundraising, and public relations.

Expectations of the board and of each member include:

  * Exerting a positive influence within the school and the university
  * Providing advice and counsel to the dean and senior staff
  * Serving as a conduit between students, faculty and administration
  * Shaping and supporting the school's position within the university
