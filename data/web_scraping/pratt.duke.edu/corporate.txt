## Get Involved with First-Year Design

Through Duke Engineering's First-Year Design (FYD) program, our students work
in multidisciplinary teams to design and build prototype solutions to
authentic, client-based problems.

Three ways to get involved:

  * Offer a project idea
  * Share your knowledge
  * Provide support
