# Andrew Douglas Hilton

Professor of the Practice in the Department of Electrical and Computer
Engineering

Drew Hilton is an Associate Professor of the Practice in Electrical and
Computer Engineering, as well as Pratt’s Director of Innovation in Computing
Education.

His main focus is on teaching professional-level programming skills to ECE’s
master's students to prepare them for software engineering careers.

Professor Hilton also teaches a 3-week introduction to Programming Python for
Duke's Master in Interdisciplinary Data Science, and Duke's Center for
Computational Thinking.

He has two Coursera specializations, one in Java, and another in C.
