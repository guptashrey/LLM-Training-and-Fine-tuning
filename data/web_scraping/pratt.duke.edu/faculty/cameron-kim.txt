# Cameron M Kim

Assistant Professor of the Practice in the Department of Biomedical
Engineering

Education advances in biomolecular/cellular engineering, mathematical modeling
of biological systems, biotechnology design, and integrating ethics in BME
curriculum. Expanding authentic research experiences in undergraduate
education.
