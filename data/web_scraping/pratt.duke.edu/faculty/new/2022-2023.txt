# Meet Our New Faculty for 2022-2023

Duke Engineering is an ambitious community where the best and brightest minds
are invited to devise creative solutions to the world’s greatest challenges

With an outstanding group of new faculty joining us in 2022-2023, we continue
to add depth and breadth to our research focus areas, while enhancing teaching
and mentorship for our students.

Learn more about the exciting work of our newest faculty members in the
profiles below.
