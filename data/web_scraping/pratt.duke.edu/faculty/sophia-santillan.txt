# Sophia Santillan

Associate Professor of the Practice in the Thomas Lord Department of
Mechanical Engineering and Materials Science

## Appointments and Affiliations

  * Associate Professor of the Practice in the Thomas Lord Department of
    Mechanical Engineering and Materials Science

## Contact Information

  * **Office Location:** 187 Hudson Hall, Box 90300, Durham, NC 27708
  * **Office Phone:** (919) 660-5339
  * **Email Address:** sophia.santillan@duke.edu

## Education

  * Ph.D. Duke University, 2007

## Research Interests

Engineering education, nonlinear systems, structural mechanics and numerical
methods.
