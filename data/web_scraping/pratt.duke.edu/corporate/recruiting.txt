# Recruiting Our Students

We help your organization access our top-quality students

Duke Engineering graduates are well-trained, inspired and creative engineers,
ready to make big impacts:

  * **Three-quarters** of our undergraduates complete industry internships
  * **More than 80 percent** of our master's students are employed full-time
    or pursuing further education within 6 months of graduation

* * *

## Career Fairs

There are opportunities at Duke for recruitment for internships and full-time
jobs throughout the year.

### TechConnect Career Networking

TechConnect, held twice a year—in September and January—engages you with
engineering and computer science students in an intimate, social environment.
Quickly target students through a comfortable networking/interviewing process.

### Duke Career Fairs

The Duke Career Center holds career fairs throughout the year, including the
annual Career & Summer Opportunities Fair each January.

* * *

## Internships

### Duke Engineering

Internships are an excellent way to tap into the outstanding student talent
Duke Engineering has to offer.

**More than 75 percent** of our undergraduates participate in an industry
internship. We are always working to expand internship opportunities for our
students.

  * Contact Kirsten Shaw to build your brand recognition at Duke Engineering
