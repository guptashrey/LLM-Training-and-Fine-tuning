# Data Science & AI Industry Affiliates

Strengthening relationships between industry and Duke

Join Duke University in developing the next generation of leaders equipped to
apply data science and artificial intelligence (AI) across industries.

Become a Duke Data Science & AI Industry Affiliate:

  * All interested organizations—large or small—are eligible to apply
  * Affiliates benefit from close relationships with Duke's outstanding data
    science, machine learning and AI faculty, and some of the brightest young
    minds in the world
  * You'll work with faculty to develop hands-on student practicum projects on
    topics important to your organization

Become an Affiliate

## Benefits for Affiliates

**\+ Work With Students on aProject Important to You  
**_Conduct an annual student practicum project shaped mutually with a Duke
project leader_

**\+ Engage With and Recruit the Next Generation of Data Science and AI
Talent** _  
 _Duke students who share your interests in applying data science and AI to
solve problems__

**\+ Complimentary Priority Access toTechConnect

**\+ Attend Affiliates Day at Duke

**\+ 15% Discount on Professional Education  
** _For all your employees on Duke Engineering's professional education

**\+ Get Connected

## Become a Duke Industry Affiliate

### Requirements

  * Be an organization actively using data science, machine learning or
    artificial intelligence, or, be one interested in getting started with
    these technologies to solve problems
  * Be committed to working with Duke to shape the next generation of data
    science and AI leaders
  * Provide financial sponsorship of a student practicum project program
    annually—We offer different levels of membership depending on your
    organization’s needs and circumstances (i.e., size, nonprofit status,
    etc.)

### Inquire Today

For applicants, there are no limits on the type or size of an affiliated
organization. Firms and organizations of all sizes—for-profit and
nonprofit—are invited to apply.
