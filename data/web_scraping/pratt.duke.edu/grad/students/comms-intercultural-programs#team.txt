# Graduate Communications and Intercultural Programs

Services and support for all engineering graduate students at Duke

Graduate Communications and Intercultural Programs is here to help all
engineering master's and PhD students with a variety of communication and
intercultural needs across all departments of the Pratt School of Engineering.
