# Career Services for Master's Students

Nearly 8 in 10 of our recent graduates started work or further education
within 6 months of graduation

The Duke Engineering Master's Career Services & Professional Development team
prepares students for success in the world of work.

### For Current Students

  * Meet the Team
  * Online Career Center
  * Make an Appointment
  * Register for Professional Development Workshops

Our experienced team provides individual & group coaching, online learning,
workshops and events, and more. Working with our coaches, Duke Engineering
master's students develop skills and build a professional community.

We're proud that nearly 8 in 10 recent graduates started work or further
education within six months of graduation. Through strong industry
connections, a global network of Duke alumni, and close ties with the Duke
University Career Center, we offer extensive networking opportunities and
access to employer recruitment events.

## Prepare for the World of Work

From one-on-one coaching to hosting major recruiting events, we offer a
diverse range of services to help get you prepared:

### Work with a Coach

#### Coaching Services

  * One-on-one career coaching (appointments and chat in MS Teams)
  * Practice Interviews
  * Weekly online office hours

#### Coaching Topics

  * Clarifying What You Want
  * Talking to People
  * Setting Up Your Search
  * Finding & Applying for Opportunities
  * Getting Feedback & Advice
  * Staying Positive & Motivated in Your Search
  * Interviewing
  * Closing the Search

Make an Appointment

### Attend Workshops & Events

We offer numerous workshops—examples include, but are not limited to:

#### Search Skills

  * Articulating What You Like & Are Good At
  * Your Confident LinkedIn Profile
  * Preparing for Virtual Career Fairs and Events
  * Negotiating Your Offer

#### Professional Skills

  * Improvisational Theater Techniques for Professional Success
  * Learning to Fail
  * On-Camera Communications
  * Visual Presentation Design & Delivery
  * Cross-Functional Leadership

See upcoming events

### Engage with Employers and Industry Professionals

Duke Engineering master’s students connect with employers at events such as:

  * **Alumni Chats** —virtual and in-person small group discussions with Duke
    graduates
  * **Employer Site Visits** —virtual and in-person visits with local
    employers
  * **Networking Events** —virtual and in-person meet-ups with Duke
    engineering and computer science students

_All events are held in compliance with Duke's public health measures._
