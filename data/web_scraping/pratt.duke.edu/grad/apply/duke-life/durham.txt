# Welcome to Durham

During your time at Duke University you'll have access to our hometown's
lively community and to the dynamic Research Triangle Region.

Take the opportunity to explore Durham and the area. You may be surprised at
how much the Bull City has to offer.

## Seeing is believing

From our temperate climate and our modern take on Southern cuisine, to our
entrepreneurial culture, Durham is a vibrant city in the heart of North
Carolina. You’ll find your pick of sports, history, food, and the arts — all
right here.

Take a quick tour

## Durham's Best – at a glance

  * **Mild climate** – Average temperature of 59 degrees, all four seasons

  * **Great location –** Less-than-a-day drives to Washington, Atlanta, and
    New York, plus direct flights to the Midwest, West Coast and
    internationally
  * **Affordability** – The cost of living in Durham is 0.3 percent _below_
    the U.S. national average
  * **Super smarts –** Forbes magazine lists us as a Top 10 Tech City,
    alongside Seattle, San Jose and Boston.
