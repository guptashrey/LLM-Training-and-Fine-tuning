# Life at Duke

Our students work in a supportive, close-knit community of researchers and
scholars, but we know there is more to life than work. On the pages in this
section you'll find a wealth of information to help you make the decision to
come to Duke and live in Durham, North Carolina. This is a great place to
work, dine, explore the outdoors and personal hobbies, enjoy the arts, and
raise a family.

Explore information about:
