  * Yoh Family Professor of Mechanical Engineering and Materials Science

  * Associate Professor of Chemistry

## Education

  * Ph.D. University of California - Berkeley, 2003

## Research Interests

Molecular mechanisms by which cells interact with materials, specifically
nanoparticles and conducting polymers, with applications in nanomedicine and
environmental exposure

## Courses Taught

  * EGR 393: Research Projects in Engineering
  * ME 391: Undergraduate Projects in Mechanical Engineering
  * ME 412: Modern Materials
  * ME 490: Special Topics in Mechanical Engineering
  * ME 555: Advanced Topics in Mechanical Engineering
