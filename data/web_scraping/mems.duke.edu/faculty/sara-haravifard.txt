## Research Interests

Exploring novel collective phenomena in quantum materials by means of neutron
and x-ray scattering techniques; Investigating quantum critical phenomena at
extreme environmental conditions of ultra-low temperature, high magnetic
field, and high pressure; Materials by Design: synthesis, single crystal
growth and characterization of correlated electron systems, quantum magnets,
topological systems, metal-organic frameworks, and high-temperature
superconductors.
