# Payne, Hoffman Named Bass Fellows

May 7, 2020

Recognizing excellence in research and undergraduate teaching

_From Duke Today_

Christine Payne, associate professor of mechanical engineering and materials
science, and Brenton Hoffman, associate professor of biomedical engineering,
are among Duke’s recently announced Bass Fellows. These chairs are reserved
for Duke professors with demonstrated excellence in research and undergraduate
instruction. Candidates are nominated by faculty and evaluated by a faculty
committee for having achieved “true excellence in both research and teaching”
and being good university citizens.
