# Engineering Faculty: Meet DARPA's Chris Bettinger

## Breadcrumb

  1. Home
  2. About
  3. Events

Apr 26

##  Wednesday, April 26, 2023 – 10:00AM to 11:00AM

Wilkinson Building, room 021 auditorium

Add to calendar »

Duke Engineering Faculty: Please join Associate Dean for Research Sharon
Gerecht on April 26 to hear a presentation by Christopher J. Bettinger,
program manager in the Biological Technologies Office (BTO) at the Defense
Advanced Research Projects Agency (DARPA). He will give the presentation
"Breakthrough Biological Technologies for Warfighter Protection" at 10:00 am
in 021 Wilkinson Auditorium. In the afternoon, Dr. Bettinger will be available
for small group meetings with faculty -- RSVP and request a small-group
session to eileen.kramer@duke.edu.

## Contact

Eileen Kramer, executive assistant  
eileen.kramer@duke.edu
