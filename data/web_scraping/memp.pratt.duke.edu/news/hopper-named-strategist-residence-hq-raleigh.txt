# Hopper named Strategist in Residence at HQ Raleigh

October 28, 2015

Hopper is the creator and instructor of “Competitive Strategy in Technology-
based Industries”, a highly successful course covering strategic analysis,
formulation, and implementation with global scope

Greg Hopper, Adjunct Professor in Duke’s Master of Engineering Management
Program, has been selected as the first Strategist in Residence at HQ Raleigh.
Hopper is the creator and instructor of “Competitive Strategy in Technology-
based Industries”, a highly successful course covering strategic analysis,
formulation, and implementation with global scope, now in its eleventh
semester.

Read the full story at WRAL TechWire.

HQ Raleigh is a 20,000 square foot shared workspace located in Downtown
Raleigh’s Warehouse District, on a mission to foster inclusive communities of
entrepreneurs who create lasting economic and social impact. HQ Raleigh
addresses two challenges faced by entrepreneurs: building a trusted support
community and gaining access to flexible, affordable office space. The space
is currently home to over 130 companies and is set to triple its footprint by
early 2017. To learn more, please visit hqraleigh.com.
