# International students offer lesson for professors

April 25, 2014

Bridget Fletcher was recently interviewed by an NPR station in Michigan on the
challenges Chinese students face when studying in the United States for first
time. Listen to her interview.

Bridget is the author of _Jiao Shi to Classroom: Preparing for Success in the
American Graduate School Classroom_ , a guidebook specifically for Chinese
students preparing to come to the US for graduate school.
