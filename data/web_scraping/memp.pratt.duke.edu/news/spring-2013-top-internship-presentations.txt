# Spring 2013 Top Internship Presentations

April 5, 2013

Professor John Nicholson recently named the Spring 2013 Top Presenters for the
Internship Assessment course, EGRMGMT 551. Students were treated to lunch at
the Washington Duke Inn on Friday, April 5, where each had the opportunity to
discuss their internship experiences and career and professional planning with
faculty and staff.

Congratulations to these outstanding students!

  * Jaime Andaluz
  * Eunice Choi
  * Asbjorn Jonsson
  * Chantalle Le
  * Mandisa Mazibuko
  * Ramya Ramaswamy
  * Arpit Sethi
