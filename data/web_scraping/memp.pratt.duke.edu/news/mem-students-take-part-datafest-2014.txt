# MEM Students Take Part in DataFest 2014

May 7, 2014

Several MEM students competed in Duke’s DataFest2014 event March 21-23. A
total of 21 teams of over 100 students from Duke, UNC, NC State, and other
schools participated in the event, where an organization provides an original
data set to each team. Students have fewer than 48 hours to work with the data
and present their findings to a panel of judges. Learn more about the event or
read the FiveThirtyEight.com article on the event.

MEM Student Teams included:
