# Orville Pierson, Corporate Director of Program Design and Service Delivery,
Lee Hecht Harrison

January 27, 2008

Highly Effective Job Search: Performance-Based Approach to Job Hunting

Orville Pierson is the author of The Unwritten Rules of the Highly Effective
Job Search, published by McGraw-Hill. He is currently writing a new book on
networking in job search. He is also Senior Vice President, Corporate Director
of Program Design and Service Delivery for Lee Hecht Harrison (LHH), a $200
million career services company with 240 offices worldwide. LHH is wholly
owned by Adecco SA, a global employment services firm with 5800 offices in 68
countries.

Orville creates books, Websites and videos to teach career management and job
search to LHHs clients, who number up to 100,000 per year. He also trains
career consultants. With LHH since 1992, he is the sole or principal author of
dozens of LHH publications used with the firms private clients, including
Executive Directions: Creating Options in Life and Work, Managing Your Search
Project, The Road to Entrepreneurship and Whats Next? A Road Map for
Exploring the Rest of Your Life.

Prior to joining LHH, Orville created the ground-breaking job search
assistance methodologies called the Pierson Method and Job Search Work Teams.
Earlier, he worked for other career services firms.

Orville has been in career services since 1977 as a program and process
designer, author, speaker and facilitator as well as career coach to hundreds
of private clients. His education includes a BA from Yale University and
certification in psychological instruments used in career assessment.
