# Dr. La Tondra Murray wins 2013 McDonald Award

April 29, 2013

Dr. La Tondra Murray is the 2013 recipient of the Capers and Marion McDonald
Award for Excellence in Mentoring and Advising. This award honors members of
the Pratt Engineering Community who build a better future through excellence
in mentoring and advising students and colleagues. Congratulations, Dr.
Murray!
