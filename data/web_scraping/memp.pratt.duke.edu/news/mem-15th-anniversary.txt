# MEM 15th Anniversary

October 30, 2012

This year marks the 15th anniversary of the MEM program. In honor of this
anniversary, the MEMP team travelled the country and celebrated with you, our
alumni!

The Triangle, NC - October 25th, 2012  
  
With alums representing the classes of 2000 to 2012, the event was a great way
to kick off this special tour!  
  
Boston, MA – November 27, 2012  
A group of 9 alumni joined Dr. Glass for an evening of great discussion and a
delicious meal!  
Washington, DC – November 27, 2012  
It was great seeing everyone up in Claredon! Individuals from the class of
2004 to 2012 were in attendance.  
Richmond, VA - November 28, 2012  
Thank you to our 5 local alumni who joined Jenny and Carrie for a great dinner
in Innsbrook.  
Miami, FL – January 28th, 2013  
  
Thanks for joining us for a great dinner under the stars!  
  
Orlando, FL – January 29th, 2013  
  
Alumni from 2008, 2010, and 2012 were wonderful hosts in a magical city!  
  
Seattle, WA - February 6th, 2013  
  
A great in Seattle with alumni spanning 7 years of the program!  
  
Nashville, TN – February 21st, 2013  
  
Thanks for joining us everyone! Music City was wonderful as always!  
  
Las Vegas, NV – March 7th, 2013  
  
It was great to catch up with our dMEMP alumni out in the desert!  
  
Dallas, TX – March 15th, 2013  
  
Dr. Murray had a great time in the heart of Texas!  
  
Houston, TX – March 19th, 2013  
  
A night of sharing and networking was a great way to finish the week in
Houston!  
  
San Francisco, CA – April 3rd, 2013  
  
A wonderful turnout at the Tied House in Mountain View! Thank you all for
attending!  
  
Philadelphia, PA – April 23rd, 2013  
  
The "City of Brotherly Love" welcomed us with open arms! Thanks to all the
alumni who came out.  
  
New York, NY -- April 27th, 2013  
  
Thanks for a great turn out all! Over 30 strong in NYC!  
  
Chicago, IL – June 12th, 2013  
  
Thanks for braving the weather to join us everyone!  
  
Atlanta, GA – June 24, 2013 at 6:30 PM  
  
Thanks for joining us!  
  
Charlotte, NC – November 12, 2013 at 7:00pm  
  
Thank you for a great time!
