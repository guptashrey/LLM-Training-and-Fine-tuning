# The MacGyver Cure for Cancer

May 29, 2014

With the help of MEM alumni Theo Tam and Gauravjit Singh, Dr. David Walmer of
the Duke Global Health Institute introduces the world to the "MacGyver cure
for cancer”.

Read more about Theo's experience in his 2007 write-up of winning the $100,000
CUREs Business Plan Competition to start a non‐profit company, ImaGyn, or the
original press release announcing the win.
