# MEM Professor wins IBM Faculty Award

February 14, 2013

MEM Professor John Nicholson recently received an **IBM Faculty Award** to
fund an EGRMGMT556 consulting practicum on "Using Social Business Software in
a University Setting."

The MEM practicum is a semester-long course presenting a team of five or six
students a business problem to solve, providing a real-world consulting
experience culminating in a final presentation and report given to company
executives.

Congratulations, John!
