# Duke MEM Online Ranked No. 2 Online Graduate Program for Engineering
Management

February 4, 2021

U.S. News has ranked Duke MEM Online as the No. 2 online graduate program in
engineering management in the nation.

Noting its convenience for working professionals, U.S. News has ranked Duke
MEM Online as the No. 2 online graduate program in engineering management in
the nation.

Duke MEM is also notable for campus residencies that allow online students to
connect with each other, faculty, and campus students, all while taking part
in seminars/workshops.

The Master of Engineering Management can be completed online with residencies
through Duke in two years.
