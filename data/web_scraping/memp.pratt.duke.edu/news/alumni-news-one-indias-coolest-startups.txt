# Alumni News: One of India's Coolest Startups

September 22, 2013

MEM Alum Srikanth Chunduri's startup, Emart Solutions, has been covered by
Business Today as one of the Coolest Startups of 2013 in India. In a recent
post in The Economic Times, Vivek Wadhwa called his company GoVasool.com,
"India's Amazon.com".

Chunduri writes, "We couldn't afford to be in the stealth mode any longer with
200+ clients!"
