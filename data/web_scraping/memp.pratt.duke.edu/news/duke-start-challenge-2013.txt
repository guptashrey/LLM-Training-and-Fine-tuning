# Duke Start-Up Challenge 2013

March 22, 2013

Voting is now open for Round 2 on the Duke Start-Up Challenge Facebook Page!

  * Visit the Duke Start-Up Challenge homepage and click on the team names to
    watch their video pitch, or see the videos below.
  * Open to anybody with a Facebook account; each person can vote once per
    track.
  * Your votes count for 20% of the Round 2 score.
  * Voting ends on 3/27 at 12 noon ET.

Please support our Master of Engineering Management and Master of Engineering
Teams!
