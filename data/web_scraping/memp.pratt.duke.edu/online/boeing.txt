# Boeing—Learning Together Program

## Welcome, Boeing Employees!

Duke University is proud to be part of the **Learning Together Program** ,
where Boeing engineers earn Duke's Master of Engineering Management degree in
just two years through Duke MEM Online.

## Degree Requirements

To receive the Master of Engineering Management (MEM), online students
complete:

  * 3 One-Week On-Campus Residencies
  * Four core management courses
  * Four technical elective courses

Students take two courses per semester/four courses per year. The program may
be extended on a case-by-case basis.

> Duke MEM Online students take the same classes and earn the same degree as
> students in our campus program.
