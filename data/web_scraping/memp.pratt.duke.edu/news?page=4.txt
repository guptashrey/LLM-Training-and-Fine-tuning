### Making Real Impact: Duke Engineering Professional Master's Graduates

Duke Engineering's December 2022 professional master's graduates are prepared
for careers of deep meaning and broad impact

### 5th Annual MEMPC PriSim Business War Games Competition

Duke is proud to annouce this year's teams competing in the 5th annual MEMPC

May 24, 2016 | **_Duke Engineering News_**

### Alumni Spotlight: MEMP '12 Alumnus Muhammad Anwar Ul Haq Selected for the
World Energy Council's Future Energy Leaders' Program

As a Future Energy Leader, Anwar will tackle some of the world's most complex
energy problems in a community of the next generation of energy leaders.

February 15, 2016 | **_Duke Engineering News_**

### D-MEMP Student Alexander Lavin Makes Forbes "30 Under 30" List

Lavin is researching artificial intelligence and natural language processing
at Numenta in California while working his way through the management program.

January 01, 2016

### Cocks, Glass, and Fox Make Faculty List

Not one, but three Pratt faculty members were recognized on the list of “20
Engineering Management Professors You Should Know” by Online Engineering
Programs.

December 15, 2015

### Egger Coursera Offering ranked #2 in Online Courses

Business Insider has ranked Duke Professor Daniel Egger’s Coursera offering,
Mastering Data Analysis in Excel, #2 on its 2015 most popular free online
courses for professionals.

December 12, 2015

### Duke Alums team up for Superhero Startup

Jaineel Aga, MEMP '09, and Sumit Shah, Fuqua '12, teamed up to create a
superhero merchandising startup that has raised about $500,000 in Pre-Series A
Funding

November 12, 2015

### Sahillioglu wins Accenture Innovation Challenge

With his team, Erdem found and developed an innovative “Life-saving Safety
App” idea using the Internet of Things technologies that would create value
for the society and Accenture’s clients.

November 06, 2015

### Staff Repeats Duke Halloween Contest Win

Taking a cue from the popular board game, Guess Who?, staff from the Pratt
School of Engineering’s Professional Masters Programs posed as the game’s
characters

October 28, 2015

### Hopper named Strategist in Residence at HQ Raleigh

Hopper is the creator and instructor of “Competitive Strategy in Technology-
based Industries”, a highly successful course covering strategic analysis,
formulation, and implementation with global scope

September 24, 2015

### 2015 Alum a finalist in the Duke Startup Challenge

Aditya Sharma, a 2015 Duke MEMP graduate, is among ten finalists competing for
the grand prize of $50,000 in the 2015 Duke Startup Challenge finale.
