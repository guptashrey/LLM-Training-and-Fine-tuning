# Career Coach Online Office Hours for Engineering Master's Students

Apr 17

##

Monday, April 17, 2023 - 11:00am to 12:30pm

Online

Add to calendar »

## Presenter

Career Services and Professional Development

All engineering Master's students are invited to use this weekday service to
have an individual conversation with a career coach about any topic. No
appointment is needed and the conversation length will vary based on the
number of students waiting. Login directions are available at:
https://waitwhile.com/welcome/officehours The CSPD Events calendar lists
schedule updates and cancellations:
http://calendar.duke.edu/index?gf%5B%5D=Engineering+Master%E2%80%99s+Career+Services+and+Professional+Development/
The EMP Career Coach Alerts channel has an active post alerting you whether
office hours are open for business:
https://teams.microsoft.com/l/channel/19%3aJ8Z343okAfaDgExPkqbo-
TE52499Va2l7kB1DVtd5cc1%40thread.tacv2/General?groupId=375bcbdc-f0fb-41dc-a39a-47595f7c4613&tenantId=cb72c54e-4a31-4d9e-b14a-1ea36dfac94c
Also, check out the CSPD website. There's lots to help you if office hours
aren't currently open:
https://careerhub.students.duke.edu/channels/engineering-masters/
