## Directions

Our offices are in Suite 3120 Fitzpatrick Center, at Duke University in
Durham, North Carolina USA

  * Driving Directions »
  * Parking and transit options »
  * Ground directions from nearest public parking (0.25 miles, total):
    * From the Bryan Center Parking Garage exit, head northeast about 100 feet, and cross Bryan Center Parking Lot
    * Turn left and head toward Science Drive, continue about 150 feet
    * Turn right onto Science Drive, continue about 350 feet
    * Enter Harrington Engineering Quad walkway, and continue about 440 feet
    * Turn right toward the Fitzpatrick Center courtyard, and continue about 85 feet
    * Turn left at entry doors. Enter the east wing of the Fitzpatrick Center
    * Head right and cross the public seating area to arrive at the main stairway or elevators, about 100 feet
    * Go up to Floor 3
    * From the stairway or elevators, turn right
    * Continue down the corridor to Suite 3120
