# Graduate Career Outcomes

Nearly three-quarters of graduates are employed or pursuing further education
within 6 months of graduation

Duke Engineering's dedicated Master's Career Services and Professional
Development team supports all Duke MEM students with internship and job
searches, including career skills training, building professional
relationships, and exploring opportunities with top organizations.
