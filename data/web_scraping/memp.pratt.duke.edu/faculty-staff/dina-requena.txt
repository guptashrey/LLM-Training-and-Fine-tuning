# Dina Requena

Executive in Residence in the Pratt School of Engineering

My expertise lies on helping organizations (high tech & life sciences)
generate product strategies and plans where their product portfolio can be
delivered to market and managed efficiently by using adequate processes
(DevOps, Agile, Waterfall, Hybrid) tailored to their specific industry needs
with strong focus on improving enterprise level operational efficiencies for
go to market agility. I bring technology development and management experience
from my corporate assignments at IQVIA, Lenovo, Toshiba, IBM, and my own cloud
computing startup. I have led large and complex global teams in successfully
delivering new technologies to market while meeting or exceeding business
objectives. As an Executive in Residence at Duke, I spearheaded the creation
of the Software Management Track so that students are more prepared for a
world that is heavily reliant on software applications.

Courses taught:  
\- Managing Product Development  
\- Project Management  
\- Software Engineering Management  
\- Software Business Management  
\- Software, Solutions & Enterprise Architecture

Research Areas:  
\- Gaming as learning platform for STEM education  
\- Applying web 3.0 in the industry
