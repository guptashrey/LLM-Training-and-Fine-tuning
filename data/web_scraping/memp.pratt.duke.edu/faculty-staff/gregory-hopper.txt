# Gregory Hopper

Adjunct Associate Professor in the Pratt School of Engineering

Greg Hopper is a strategist, educator, and executive focused on creating
customer value. He is CEO of Strategic Edge Executive Resources, LLC, a
business growth and strategy firm in Raleigh, NC, and lead strategist for the
Executive Farm Management Program, a joint effort of NCSU, ECU, and Clemson.
Greg’s career started at IBM, includes co-founding a venture-backed startup,
and extensive experience in management consulting in a broad spectrum of
industries. He is a Senior Member of the IEEE, member of the Product
Development and Management Association, and faculty at Duke Corporate
Education. His undergraduate degree is in electrical engineering from
Lafayette College, and earned a Masters in Entrepreneurship from Western
Carolina University. Greg holds 14 US and international patents.
