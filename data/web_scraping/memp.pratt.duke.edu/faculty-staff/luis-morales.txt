# Luis Morales

Executive Director, Master of Engineering Management Program

Luis Morales has 28 years of professional experience in various engineering
leadership roles at Cisco Systems and AT&T Labs. In his most recent
assignment, he led a Cisco team responsible for analyzing customer sentiment
and experience, create insights and compel the business to drive improvement
actions.

**Specialties  
**

  * Building high-performing and engaged teams and leaders
  * Passionate about quality, customer success and continuous process/product
    improvements
  * HW & SW quality data analytics
  * Thought leader in Quality Management System and customer experience
  * Customer advocacy and customer quality engagements

****

**Inventor  
** Six patents in the area of IP networking and security (one for Lucent, and
five for AT&T)—

  * US Patents: 5761203, 5987526, 6044092, 7444417, 792576 and 7953855.
