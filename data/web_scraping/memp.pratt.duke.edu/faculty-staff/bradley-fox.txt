# Bradley A. Fox

Associate Dean of Master's Programs

Dr. Fox joined Duke University in 2003. He has experience in all phases of
technology development at major corporations including Kobe Steel USA Inc, IBM
Corporation, and GE. He was awarded the 2011 IEEE Technology Management
Council's Engineering Management Educator of the Year Award.

## Contact Information

  * **Office Location:** 3405A Fitzpatrick Center
  * **Office Phone:** (919) 660-5516
  * **Email Address:** brad.fox@duke.edu

## Education

  * PhD University of Virginia
  * BS Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University

## Specialties

  * Technology Development
  * Project Management
