# Dean Hering

Adjunct Associate Professor in the Pratt School of Engineering

Dean joined the faculty after being owner and Chief Innovator at NetCentrics
for 17 years. Prior to that was the Director of the Center for Digital Systems
Engineering at RTI International. Dean has worked internationally on
innovation projects for Michelin, Volvo, John Deere, IBM, NASA, DOD and other
organizations. He has designed electronic systems, produced commercial
software, developed virtual reality experiences, and led innovation processes
for over 30 years. He holds 5 patents and works to develop student leaders.
