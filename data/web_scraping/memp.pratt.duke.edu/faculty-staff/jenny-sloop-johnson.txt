# Jenny Sloop Johnson

Assistant Director of Career Services

Jenny is Assistant Director of Career Services for Duke's Professional Masters
Programs.

Jenny received her bachelor’s degree in Accounting from Meredith College and a
Master of Education degree in Higher Education Administration from North
Carolina State University with a focus on career services and advising.

Jenny is a Directorate Member of ACPA’s Commission for Career Services as well
as a leader in the North Carolina Association of Colleges & Employers (NCACE).
In 2014, she was recognized as a _Rising Star_ in the field of Career Services
from the Commission for Career Services and received the _Furney K. James
Service Award_ from NCACE.
