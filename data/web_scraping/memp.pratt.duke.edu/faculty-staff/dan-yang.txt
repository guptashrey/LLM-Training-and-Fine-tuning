# Dan Yang

Adjunct Assistant Professor in the Pratt School of Engineering

Dan Yang is an Adjunct Assistant Professor in the Pratt School of Engineering
at Duke University. Dan has over a decade of experience working as a data and
advanced analytics expert for global leading companies such as IBM, McKinsey &
Company, and Bank of America, serving internal and external clients across
technology, healthcare, financial services, real estate, and education
industries. Dan is an alumnus of IBM’s flagship pro bono consulting programs,
including IBM Corporate Service Corps and IBM Health Corps. She received IBM
Outstanding Contributor Award, Eminence & Excellence Awards, Manager's Choice
Awards, and Bank of America Medallion Awards. She is a Distinguished Data
Scientist jointly certified by IBM and the Open Group and a Six Sigma Master
Black Belt.
