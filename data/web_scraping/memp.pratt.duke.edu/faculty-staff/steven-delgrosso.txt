# Steven DelGrosso

Adjunct Associate Professor in the Pratt School of Engineering

Steve serves as an Adjunct Associate Professor in the Pratt School of
Engineering. He teaches Project Management with an emphasis on applying
consulting principles and practices to large, complex projects and programs.
Steve also advises Duke students in the Masters of Engineering Management
Program's Operations and Supply Chain Management track.

Steve is the previous Executive Director of IBM's global Project Management
Center of Excellence and former Chair of the Project Management Institute's
(PMI) Board of Directors. He currently serves on PMI's Certification
Governance Council advising the PMI Board on items affecting professional
certification and credentialing.

Leveraging his global leadership and consulting experience, he challenges his
students to always consider the strategic, technical and business value
impacts of all recommendations and decisions.
