## Preparing engineering and science graduates for leadership

The Duke Master of Engineering Management is a tech-savvy alternative to the
MBA. Comprising a core management curriculum and variety of electives, it is:
**interdisciplinary** and **flexible** , with a focus on **application** ,
**global awareness** , and the **student experience**.

# Master of Engineering Management | Duke University

## Is Engineering Management Right for Me?

Having technical and business skills is an advantage

There is a critical need across the globe for people who can integrate
technical skills with business acumen to solve complex problems.

Duke's Master of Engineering Management graduates meet that need. The Duke
Difference »

Engineering Management might be right for you if you are:

  * A person with a science or engineering background
  * Interested in business, product management, and entrepreneurship
  * Focused on a leadership career
