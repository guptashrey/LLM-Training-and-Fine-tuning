# Master of Engineering Management Online Program

Ranked #7 by U.S. News — Best Online Engineering Management Master's Programs

## Maximum flexibility to meet your interests

Four core engineering management courses

  * Developed in partnership with Duke's Law School and Fuqua School of
    Business (12 credits)

Four technical elective courses

  * Provide additional expertise in areas of your interest (12 credits)

Three 1-Week Residencies

  * Orientation, Mid-Program, and Capstone (0 credits)

A Project Experience

  * Including written summary and oral presentation (6 credits)

Typically, the degree takes two years to complete. With approval, students may
extend the program beyond two years.

How to Apply

Contact admissions »

> Duke MEM Online students learn with the same faculty, take the same classes,
> and earn the same Duke degree as students in our on-campus Master of
> Engineering Management program.

## IS Duke MEM Online for ME?

The Duke Master of Engineering Management Online is for students who:

  * Want the same high-quality curriculum and experience offered on campus,
    but cannot interrupt their careers
  * Are highly organized and self-motivated
  * Have a supportive, flexible work environment
  * Can commit to three one-week on-campus residencies
  * Can maintain excellence in work and educational demands simultaneously

## Class Profile: Duke MEM Online

Duke MEM cohorts are small, and students connect to each other and the
university through our innovative model of online learning and campus
residencies.

### Cohort Profile

  * Cohort Size: 20-30
  * Women: 25%
  * Age at Entry (middle 50%): 24-28
  * Work Experience (middle 50%): 2-5 years

### Scores (25-75% Range)

  * GRE Verbal: 153-160
  * GRE Quantitative: 156-164
  * GRE Analytical Writing: 4.0-4.5
  * Grade Point Average: 3.1-3.6

_Note: This information was gathered from several cohorts to give prospective
students an idea of a typical class._
